WD Packaging Systems, Inc. was established in 1992 to provide the highest quality of used packaging equipment, materials and used processing machinery to the packaging, and processing industries across North America and abroad. Our company specializes in used machinery in the industries of food & beverage, fresh produce, red meats and poultry, household products and chemicals.

What we offer extends beyond packaging materials. We offer the full scope of manufacturing and packaging for your product, as well as expert consultation. That way you can focus on your brand, your product, and your goals. Leave the rest to us.
